Delicious IPA from Whylan

Beer: Chasing Mirrors

Brewed by: Wylam

Style: IPA
Date drank: 1/21/19

17.6° Plato OG 1072.2 ABV 7.1% IPA... Big, bold and creamy with a heavy dose of wheat and flakes backed up with an amalgam of new season tropical hop treats including Citra, Chinook, Centennial and Mosaic. Big,ripe and fleshy with a soft fruit fullness and a pulpy, whipped velvety finish. Now that you know what goes there, what’s to stop you taking it home?
